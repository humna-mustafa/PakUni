# Data Verification & Sources

The data in **PakUni** is curated from authoritative and verifying sources to ensure 100% accuracy for students. Below are the sources and verification methods used for each data category.

## 1. Universities
**Source:** Higher Education Commission (HEC) Pakistan
**Verification URL:** `hec.gov.pk`
**Last Verified:** Jan 2026

*   **Recognition:** All universities listed are HEC recognized.
*   **Ranking:** Rankings are based on the latest HEC Quality Assurance ranking and QS World University Rankings (Asia/Pakistan chapter).
*   **Campuses:** Verified from each university's official website.

## 2. Career Paths
**Source:** Industry Experts & Job Market Analysis
**Verification Method:** Cross-referenced with current job market trends (LinkedIn, Rozee.pk) and academic requirements.

*   **Salary Data:** Aggregated from Payscale, Glassdoor (Pakistan region), and local industry surveys for 2025-2026.
*   **Education:** Based on standard curriculum requirements by HEC and professional bodies (PMDC, PEC, ICAP, PBC).

## 3. Merit & Admission
**Source:** Official Admission Portals
**Status:** Merit formulas are standard for the 2025-2026 academic session.

*   **Medical:** PMC / PMDC regulations.
*   **Engineering:** PEC accreditation standards.

## 4. Maintenance
To maintain "100% Completeness":
1.  **Quarterly Audits:** We review admission deadlines every 3 months.
2.  **User Feedback:** The "Report Issue" feature allows students to flag outdated info immediately.
3.  **Automated Checks:** Broken links to admission portals are checked monthly.

*PakUni is committed to providing the most accurate educational data in Pakistan.*
